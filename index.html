About

Bethany studied violin with Marcia Littley and piano with Ana Lourdes Rodriguez. She has participated in local masterclasses with violinist Erzhan Kulibaev and members of the Borodin and Amernet String Quartets. She plays periodically with the Miami Chamber Orchestra and other ensembles in South Florida, performs for weddings and events, and gives private piano and violin lessons. Bethany is pursuing her Master’s in violin performance at Florida International University in Miami and studying with violinist Misha Vitenson.

Performance Highlights

Atlantic Music Festival

2023

Performed orchestral, chamber and solo repertoire and studied with violin faculty Jinjoo Cho, Kyung Sun Lee, and Yvonne Lam.

Miami Classical Music Festival
